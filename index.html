If you have logged in to MOVIQ using Facebook and wish to delete your data, please follow one of the options below:

Option 1: Request Data Deletion via Email

Step 1: Send an email request

Send an email to:

chkilisaif776@gmail.com

with the subject "Delete My Data".

Step 2: Confirmation and processing

Your Facebook user ID and all related data will be permanently deleted from our database within 7 days of your request.

Option 2: Remove Access via Facebook

You can also remove MOVIQ's access directly from your Facebook account settings:

Settings & Privacy → Apps and Websites

Find MOVIQ in the list and remove it to revoke access.
